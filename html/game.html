Mechanics:

In this game, you have to remain alive on the longest time possible. This could be done by avoiding the cars and bats that would come accross your way. You can avoid them by either swiping from one lane to another, jumping, or shooting. Ofcourse, you can't shoot the vehicles, but you ca shoot the bats. Touching the bats while jumping and bumping on another vehicle would cause you to lose.

Controls:

A - >swipe Left

D - >swipe Right

Space Bar - >Jump

Right Mouse Click - >Gun shots
